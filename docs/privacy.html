Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

The short version

  • Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer does not collect any personal data
  • All timer sessions and settings are stored locally on your device
  • We do not track your activity or send analytics
  • Your focus sessions and statistics never leave your device

Data we collect

None. Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer does not have a server, account system, or any method to collect your information.

Data stored on your device

Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer stores the following data locally on your iPhone or iPad using iOS's private storage:

  • Your timer preferences (focus duration, break duration, etc.)
  • Custom activities you create
  • Focus session history and statistics
  • Theme and appearance preferences

This data is only accessible by Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer on your device and is never shared with us or any third party.

Notifications

Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer can send you local notifications to alert you when a focus session or break ends. These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any server communication.

Third party services

Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services, or tracking tools.

Children's privacy

Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any data, there is no special consideration needed for children's privacy.

Your rights

Since Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer stores all data on your device:

  • You can delete all data by deleting the app
  • You have complete control over your data
  • No data is stored on our servers

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Immerse: Flow & Focus Timer handles data — for example, by adding optional analytics in a future version — we will update this policy and notify you through the app before the change takes effect.
